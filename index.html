Study 1 Detail

About This Gamification Study

Gamified mobile alarm app designed to help users overcome morning grogginess and break the habit of snoozing. Instead of a simple dismiss button, the alarm requires you to chase and tap a shifting "off" button multiple times.

This micro-interaction subtly measures your reaction speed and accuracy to generate a daily "Morning Alertness Score," giving you real-time feedback on how awake you truly are. The app tracks your alertness over time to provide personalized insights and help you start the day feeling more energized.
